AS LONGAS THE LEMON TREES GROW~BYZoulfa Katouh

Lemon trees are known for their endurance, and they serve as a metaphor for the persistence of life even in dire circumstances. This book tells us how a war effect people their heart, their mind.War doesn't just only destroy building and landscape, it shatters aspiration and future.It seperates a mother from her child, it seperates the two people who planned their whole life together and were waiting to be together, but were torn apart from each other. Salama, a young Syrian woman who is grappling with the aftermath of war, loss, and the challenges of living in a war-torn country. Throughout her journey, she finds herself caught between the desire to fight for a better future and the pull to survive in a world that has been irrevocably changed.
